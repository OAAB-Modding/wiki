> [!Note]
> The **Daedric Face of Revelation** represents the aspect of the Daedra Lord [[Azura]]. It is a holy artifact and only a true Champion of Azura is worthy to wear it publicly. Prove your devotion to the Goddess of Dawn and Dusk by collecting artifacts associated with her. To begin your quest, travel to the Holamayan Monastery and speak with Tivam Sadri.
