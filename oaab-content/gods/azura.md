### Priesthood of Azura
It is said that the priesthood of Azura left or were driven from Morrowind to seek "true revelation" in the [far east](https://www.lyithdonea.wiki/places/lands/lyithdonea). The Shrine of Azura on Vvardenfell was one of their last known shrines.

> [!Quote]
> The Face of Revelation is a daedric mask depicting the aspect of Azura. It is a sacred artifact and ours is the only one I know of left in Morrowind. All others were taken when Azura's priesthood left Morrowind to seek true revelation. Only a named Champion of Azura may wear the mask without suffering retribution. Tivam tells me that you are "star-blessed" and carry the token of Azura's Star.
> -- [[beraren-sadri|Beraren Sadri]] (dialogue)[^1]

> [!Quote]
> There are tunnels below the shrine. Ancient shelters used by the priesthood of Azura to hide from the Tribunal zealots before sailing to the far east. Beraren is down there, I'm sure of it. If you can retrieve the mask, it's yours. You've proven you have the right to wear it. I wish Beraren hadn’t chosen this path... we will retrieve his remains after you return.
> -- [[tivam-sadri|Tivam Sadri]] (dialogue)[^1]
### Star-blessed
Being called "star-blessed" indicates some relationship to Azura.

> [!Quote]
> ...Star-blessed hand wields thrice-cursed blade...
> ... Line 6-7: 'Star-blessed' suggests Azura, the Daedra Lord and patron of magic, fate, and prophecy.
> -- [The Lost Prophecy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:The_Lost_Prophecy)

> [!Quote]
> Welcome to our monastery, %PCName. They say you are star-blessed. Blessed by Azura. You should visit the Shrine of Azura and seek the favor of Azura. I would talk with you further, but only after you have served the Queen of Dawn and Dusk.
> -- [[tivam-sadri|Tivam Sadri]] (dialogue)[^1]
